THE ARCHITECT

Aldous Clark Batolena

Licensed Architect

Clark finished his degree at National University and has garnered more than ten years of experience in design conceptualization and architectural planning.

As an architect, he manages the design team’s schedule, processes and manpower allocation to meet the project’s required level of service. Clark is also a full-time Senior Architect handling various projects types with varying complexities.

Affiliations: Member, United Architects of the Philippines Manila Centrum Chapter

THE DESIGNER

Sarah Louise Batolena

Licensed Interior Designer

Louise started her career as a draftswoman then as a kitchen designer and on to racking up two decades of commercial and hospitality interior design experience. Her dedication to her craft, her focused approach, and the passion in her work, has have been the driving force to find challenge in her work with international firms in the Philippines and in Qatar.

As head of the Interior Design department, she provides the design direction to a dedicated team of interior designers and architects working on interior design projects of SSI retail stores, Ayala Land Inc., Federal Land Inc., Buddha Bar Manila, and the Security Bank Centre.

Affiliations: Member, Philippine Institute of Interior Designers
